Who Am I?

Hi I'm Crescens, an aspiring MERN developer
I hold a professional certificate in MERN full-stack and a bachelors in Cyber Security. Information Technology (IT) has always been my interest as logic and understanding how things works excites me.

With my passion in IT and helping others, I want to create a seamless and enriching world for others. Though I'm not the next Steve Jobs but in the little ways, I want to empower others through making impactful applications.

While taking my Diploma in Information Technology, I was volunteering for an events company and was manning a signup booth. The process of signing up was tedious and manual, naturally being an IT student, I thought of the ways technology could have lightened this burden. This sparked my interest for automation. I have yet to try out automation but in the near future, I want to veer into the world of automation and enrich people's day to day life.
